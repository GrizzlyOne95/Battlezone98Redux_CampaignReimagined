On IO, we descended into the depths of the abyss with orders to bring it out. It. What was it? I knew found relic told us about it, but nobody outside S&T knew for sure what it was. It was rumored to be a new technology upgrade resulting from the biomedical research the cathonians performed on Venus. They called it project furies. All we really knew for sure was that the Soviets were going to make a play for it. So we had to do the same. The race goes on.
